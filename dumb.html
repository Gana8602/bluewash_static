Service Delivery Policy

Introduction

At [Your Car Wash Service], we are committed to providing high-quality, on-site car wash services. This Service Delivery Policy outlines how our services are scheduled, delivered, and any relevant terms and conditions.

Service Scheduling

Our services are available by appointment. You can schedule a service through our website, by phone, or by email. When scheduling, you will need to provide:

  • Your name
  • Contact information (phone number and email address)
  • Home address or location where the service will be performed
  • Preferred date and time for the service
  • Details about your vehicle (make, model, and any specific requests)

Once your appointment is scheduled, you will receive a confirmation email or text message with the details of your booking.

Service Delivery

On the scheduled date and time, our professional car wash team will arrive at your specified location to perform the service. Please ensure that your vehicle is accessible and that any necessary preparations are made (e.g., removing personal items from the vehicle).

If you need to reschedule or cancel your appointment, please contact us at least 24 hours in advance. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the scheduled service time may be subject to a cancellation fee.
