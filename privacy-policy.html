Privacy Policy

Introduction

Blue Wash. We values your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email Address

  • Vehicle Information

Payment Information

  • Credit/Debit Card Details
  • Billing Information

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

  • To process and confirm your bookings.
  • To dispatch our employees to your location for car washing services.

Payment Processing

  • To process payments for our services.

Customer Support

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support.

Communication

  • To send you updates, promotions, and information about our services.

Improvement of Services

  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our website and services.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

Service Providers

  • We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our business, such as payment processors and IT service providers.

Legal Requirements

  • We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Security of Your Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet or email transmission is ever fully secure or error-free. Please keep this in mind when disclosing any information to us via the internet.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of any incorrect or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal obligations.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your information for certain purposes.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
